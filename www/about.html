Quick Banking v1.6.7

USSD - Unstructured Supplementary Service Data.


Quick Banking is a mobile application which aims to simplify and fasten USSD banking.
A complete list of Nigerian Banks that offer banking services via USSD is available at a glance.
Further clicking on a particular bank will reveal all USSD codes/services available from the bank.
Some USSD features may require user input.
